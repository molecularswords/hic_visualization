Hi-C visualization

Click on an area of the plot for a detailed view of the interactions between two chromosomes for the growth state you wish to see. In the detailed view the range of the scaling can be decreased or increased by mousiing over the plot and scrolling up or down, respectively, or by pressing the appropriate scale buttons. Double click or press the appropriate button to return to the overview.
Performance note: Please use Chrome or Safari for this visualization. Firefox is terribly slow at rendering a large number of SVG elements simultaneously and will thus provide a very bad experience.
